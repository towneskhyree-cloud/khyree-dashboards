The recommendation.

Four directions. Real range. None of them lean on the acid-green default. Now the design question becomes a positioning question: how do you want the city to see you?

BROADCAST says I'm an event. Hype, energy, attention. Burns hot.
EDITORIAL says I'm a craftsman. Slow, considered, profound. Builds trust.
HERITAGE says I have roots. Family, lineage, return. The most "Khyree City" of the four.
MAISON says I'm above the market. Membership, not transaction. Highest ceiling.

— if I had to pick one —

Lead with HERITAGE. It's the most authentic to who you actually are — a barber returning to a family shop, carrying something forward. Use MAISON for the founding-20 offer (matches the "membership" framing). Use BROADCAST sparingly, only for hype-drop moments. Keep EDITORIAL in your back pocket for press features and year-end reflections.
