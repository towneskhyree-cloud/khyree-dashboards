SHEET A-01 · DIAGNOSTIC · SITE PLAN

Where most barbers
get stuck.

Before you build, look at the lot. Four traps account for 95% of barber career stagnation. Most operators die inside one of them and never see the road out. The blueprint begins with locating yours.

YOU ARE HERE
ZONE 01
Booth Purgatory
You rent a chair from someone whose name is on the door. You pay $200–400/wk for the privilege. They take walk-ins on slow days. Every Friday feels like rent day.
Avg time stuck · 3–7 yrs
ZONE 02
The 60/40 Split
You give the shop 40% of every ticket. They keep your clients on their system. The day you leave, the calendar leaves with them. Equity in someone else's brand.
Avg time stuck · 4–10 yrs
ZONE 03
The Side Hustle Trap
Cutting nights and weekends from your kitchen. No tax structure. No reviews. No upgrade ladder. The ceiling is hard and the income is grey.
Avg time stuck · 2–5 yrs
ZONE 04
The Burnout Shop
You bought into the volume game. 60 cuts a week at $35. No tier, no upsell, no Reserve service. The body breaks before the books do.
Avg time stuck · 5–15 yrs

The exit out of every zone is the same gate: own your book, own your time, own your tier. The blueprint takes you to the gate. You walk through it.

Most barbers don't realize they're in Zone 01. They think Zone 01 is the industry.
SHEET S-02 · STRUCTURAL · FOUNDATION

The foundation.
Mindset. Money. Identity.

Skip this sheet and the whole building leans. The barbers who collapse at month 6 don't fail because the cuts got worse — they fail because the foundation was never poured. Three loads bear on this slab.

FIG. 02 · STRUCTURE — ELEVATION
01

The Money Identity

You're a small business, not a service worker. Day 1 forms an LLC. Day 2 opens a business checking. Day 3 every dollar moves through it. CPA on retainer by month 3.

SLAB · BASE
02

The Two Ledgers

Client book = the asset that travels with you. Business book = the asset that buys you out. Most barbers track neither. You'll track both, weekly, with one A4 sheet.

SLAB · MID
03

The Hourly Truth

What you charge per cut means nothing without what you earn per hour. Real math: ticket × cuts per day × days per week ÷ working hours = your real wage. Floor: $80/hr. Goal: $150/hr.

SLAB · TOP
04

The Time Block

A booked chair is a profit center. An empty chair is rent. Calendars are the difference. Default cadence: 45-min Authentic, 60-min Hero, 75-min Reserve. Buffer: 10 min.

FOOTING
05

The Identity Lock

You don't sell haircuts. You sell what someone feels walking out the door. Decide the feeling first. The rest of the blueprint is in service of it.

REBAR · WHOLE

This sheet is non-negotiable. You can have the sharpest line in the city and still go broke at the end of the month if the foundation cracks. Money identity → ledger discipline → hourly truth → time block → identity lock. In that order.

SHEET M-04 · MECHANICAL · WIRING

The wiring.
Systems that run while you sleep.

Six automation circuits every Level-Up chair runs. Set up once. They earn the upcharge, fill the slot, recover the no-show, ask for the review. The barber who has these doesn't work harder — they work later in the funnel.

Circuit 03 · ARRIVE

Circuit 04 · REBOOK
IN-CHAIR
Next slot offered before the cape comes off. Loyalty becomes habit, not hope.
Circuit 05 · REVIEW

Circuit 06 · RE-ENGAGE
D+35

Circuit 07 · REFER
3×

Circuit 08 · MEMORY
∞
Profile note per client. "Two on top, faded sides, no oil." The memory IS the luxury.

The Khyree City booking app at book.khyreecity.com already has Circuits 01, 02, 04, 05, 08 live as of v13. Circuit 06 + 07 ship in v14. This is the platform we open up to Level-Up Tier 1 members in Phase 2 — branded sub-pages, same circuits, $50/mo.

SHEET O-10 · OPERATIONS · RUNWAY

90 days to ignition.
