Rich Dad's Cash Flow Patterns

THE THREE DIAGRAMS — NO NUMBERS, JUST THE ARROWS
1. The Poor
Money in. Money out. Nothing left.
2. The Middle Class
More income. More liabilities. Treadmill.
3. The Rich
Buy assets. Assets feed income. Loop.
The One Rule
An asset puts money IN your pocket.
A liability takes money OUT of your pocket.

That's it. That's the whole book in two lines. Most people call their house an asset. Rich dad called it a liability — because every month it pulls money out (mortgage, taxes, repairs, insurance). The diagrams show what direction the arrows go. The arrows don't lie.
